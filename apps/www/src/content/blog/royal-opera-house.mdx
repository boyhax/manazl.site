# Royal Opera House Muscat: Where Culture Meets Elegance

The Royal Opera House Muscat (ROHM) stands as a testament to Oman's commitment to arts and culture, blending traditional Omani architecture with contemporary design.

## Architectural Marvel

![Royal Opera House Exterior](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1703659305541-9adbe1441df3?q=80&w=1470&auto=format&fit=crop&ixlib=rb-4.0.3&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D)

The building features:
- Stunning white marble façade
- Traditional Omani architectural elements
- Modern theatrical technology
- 1,100 seating capacity

> "The Royal Opera House Muscat represents the vision of cultural excellence in the Gulf region, showcasing both Eastern and Western artistic traditions."

## Planning Your Visit
