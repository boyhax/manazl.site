# Top 10 Must-Visit Places in Muscat

Muscat, the capital of Oman, combines traditional Arabian charm with modern development. Here are the must-visit places that showcase the city's rich heritage and contemporary life.

## 1. Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque

The crown jewel of Omani architecture, this magnificent mosque features:

- World's largest hand-woven carpet
- Crystal chandeliers
- Marble walls with intricate Islamic patterns
- Beautiful gardens and fountains

> "The mosque's main prayer hall can accommodate over 6,500 worshippers, making it one of the largest in the region."

## 2. Mutrah Souq

One of the oldest markets in the Arab world, Mutrah Souq offers:

- Traditional Omani silver and gold jewelry
- Frankincense and exotic spices
- Hand-woven textiles and carpets
- Local handicrafts and antiques

### Best Time to Visit
- Morning: 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Evening: 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM

## 3. Royal Opera House Muscat

A masterpiece of contemporary Omani architecture that hosts:

- World-class opera performances
- Classical music concerts
- Traditional Omani music shows
- Ballet and theater productions

![Royal Opera House](/images/blog/royal-opera-house.jpg)

## 4. Al Alam Palace

The ceremonial palace of His Majesty Sultan Haitham bin Tariq features:

- Stunning blue and gold columns
- Traditional Omani architecture
- Beautiful gardens
- Historic significance

## 5. Bait Al Zubair Museum

Learn about Omani heritage through:

- Traditional clothing and jewelry
- Ancient weapons and household items
- Historical photographs
- Regular cultural exhibitions
