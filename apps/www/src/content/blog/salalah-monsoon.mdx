# Salalah: A Green Paradise During Khareef

Salalah, the second-largest city in Oman, transforms into a lush green paradise during the Khareef (monsoon) season. Here's your complete guide to experiencing this natural wonder.

## When to Visit

The Khareef season typically runs from:
- **Start**: Late June
- **Peak**: July to August
- **End**: Early September

> "Salalah during Khareef is like stepping into a different world - where desert meets tropical paradise."
